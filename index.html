About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I'm a 1st year graduate student at NUS. Currently, my primary research interest lies in the mathematical understanding and algorithm designs for sampling and generative models, with an emphasize on score-based generative models.


I am also broadly intrigued by the following topics:

  • Connections between sampling and physics (e.g. field theory)
  • Machine learning in general
  • Bayesian statistics
  • Application of ML and sampling to physical sciences and scientific computing (weather, neuroscience, PDE etc.)
  • Large language models
  • Quantum computing

My programming skills include C++, Python, Julia and LATEX.


Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss any of these topics! You can call me Yang, which sounds like "young" in English.
