Hobbies

Gaming

I enjoy exploring a variety of video games in my spare time, organized into three main categories, though they sometimes overlap:

Souls Series (e.g., Dark Souls, Elden Ring, Sekiro): I'm drawn to their atmospheric worlds, intricate and dark lore, and the rewarding challenge of learning from failure.

Games with Complex 3D Maps and Innovative Mechanics (e.g., Outer Wilds, Control, Portal 2): I find joy in exploring their intricate environments and experimenting with creative gameplay mechanics.

Space and Sci-Fi Games (e.g., Deliver Us The Moon, Elite Dangerous, Mass Effect): These games offer a chance to experience today what scientists and engineers are diligently working on to realize tomorrow.
